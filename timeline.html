Long-Term Relationship Timeline

  • 22nd Jan - 10th of March, 2024

    Registration for mentors is open

    Mentors can apply for Long-Term Mentorship Relationships till 10th of March. Once a profile has been approved and published on the website, the mentor will receive a confirmation email.
  • 22nd of February - 20th of March, 2024

    Registration for mentees is open

    Mentees will have an opportunity to choose a mentor and apply for Long-Term Mentorship Relationships till 20th of March.
  • 12th of March, 2024

    Panel Discussion “Challenges Faced by Women in Technology and How Mentors Can Help?”

    The discussion will cover topics such as specific challenges that women in technology often encounter and how mentorship can provide support and guidance.

  • 21th March - 1st of April, 2024

    Matching process

    Once the application period has ended, the Mentorship Programme Team will review the applications. Then list of selected mentees will be sent to the mentors, along with their profiles and information about their goals and expectations for the mentorship. Based on the availability mentors will be able to accept mentees.
  • 11th of April, 2024

    Introduction emails

    Once list with matched mentors and mentees is ready, all participants will receive introduction email with further steps.
  • 11th of April, 2024

    Kick-off meeting

    Kick-off meeting: once mentors and mentees have been selected and matched, there will be a kick-off meeting and orientation to introduce the program, set expectations
  • 12-20th of April, 2024

    First meetings

    The mentor and mentee will start schedule their first meeting to get to know each other and set goals for the mentorship
  • April - November, 2024

    Regular meetings

    Mentors and mentees will schedule regular meetings to discuss progress and share advice and feedback. The frequency of these meetings will depend on the preferences of the mentor and mentee, but they will typically happen every 1-2 weeks
  • July and October, 2024

    Programme check-ins

    Two times during the cycle mentors and mentees will be invited to a mentorship check-in meeting to assess progress and collect feedback
  • November, 2024

    End of the Programme

    The mentor and mentee will agree in a final meeting to conclude the
    Mentorship Programme Team will conduct the final meeting for all mentors and mentees to wrap up the mentorship programme and discuss outcomes.

  • November and December, 2024

    Programme evaluation and feedback

    After the programme has ended, mentors and mentees will provide feedback on the programme and suggest ways to improve for future
    This can take a few weeks.
